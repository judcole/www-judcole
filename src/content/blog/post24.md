Important / momentous news on the Debian developers mailing list - they are enforcing reproducibility on all packages going forward:

"Aided by the efforts of the Reproducible Builds project, we've decided it's
time to say that Debian must ship reproducible packages. Since yesterday, we
have enabled our migration software to block migration of new packages that
can't be reproduced or existing packages (in testing) that regress in reproducibility."

[Read more](https://lists.debian.org/debian-devel-announce/2026/05/msg00001.html)
